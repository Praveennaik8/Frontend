Team forming rules

Cricket


  1. There must be 11 players.
  2. There must be one Captain and Vice Captain.
  3. There must be one wicketKeeper.
  4. There must be at least 3 and at most 5 Bowlers.
  5. There must be at least 3 and at most 5 Batsmen.
  6. There must be at least 1 and at most 3 All-Rounders.
  7. Total Points should be less than 100.


Football


  1. There must be 11 players.
  2. There must be one Captain and Vice Captain.
  3. There must be one GoalKeeper.
  4. There must be at least 4 and at most 5 Defence players.
  5. There must be at 4 and at most 6 Attacking players.
  6. Total Points should be less than 100.
